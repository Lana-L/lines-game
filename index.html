Click on a ball to activate it. Click somewhere else to move it.

Make a line of five or more balls in a row. The lines have to be straight, but can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.

Every line of five balls or longer will disappear and give you two points per ball (so 10 points minimum). You can make longer lines by inserting final balls into the middle of a line.

Every turn that you don't make a complete line, three more balls will appear.
